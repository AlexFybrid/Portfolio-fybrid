    About

    "Drdoggy" is a created visual concept of the main page, pet store. It is designed to demonstrate the possibilities of the designer. Chastically selected colors and developed the structure of the landing page

    TASKS

    1. Enhance User Experience (UX):

      • Develop intuitiveness and ease of navigation, ensuring users can find information quickly. Improve interaction with site elements to improve usability and minimize errors on the homepage


    2. Creating an attractive and modern user interface (UI):

      • Develop a website UI that is stylish and aesthetically pleasing and perfectly aligned with Drdoggy's brand identity. Utilizing modern design trends and elements allowed us to create an original and unforgettable visual identity for the site.

    3. Increase of conversion rate:

      • Develop page layouts based on conversion-focused design principles to optimize successful user interactions such as orders, and. Reinforce visually appealing elements that attract attention and motivate users to take desired actions.

    4. Develop User flow :

      • Develop the possible user flow of such a site

    Target audience

    Owners of different types of pets (probably aged 12-50) who are interested in quality and stylish accessories for their pets. Special consideration is given to people who are actively concerned about their pet's health and style
