    About

    "The Academy is a project designed to attract users to our course and increase conversion rates. We've created 10 screens that combine visual appeal and informative content to deliver a compelling user experience. The color scheme was carefully chosen to evoke associations with wealth, tranquility, and monkeys.

    TASKS

    1. Enhance User Experience (UX):

      • Develop intuitiveness and ease of navigation, ensuring users can find information quickly. Improve interaction with site elements to improve usability and minimize errors on the homepage


    2. Creating an attractive and modern user interface (UI):

      • Develop a website UI that is stylish and aesthetically pleasing and perfectly aligned with Academy brand identity. Utilizing modern design trends and elements allowed us to create an original and unforgettable visual identity for the site.

    3. Increase of conversion rate:

      • Develop page layouts based on conversion-focused design principles to optimize successful user interactions such as orders, and. Reinforce visually appealing elements that attract attention and motivate users to take desired actions.

    4. Develop User flow :

      • Develop the possible user flow of such a site

    Target audience

    This course is geared towards individuals between the ages of 20 and 35 who are driven to enhance their financial situation and yearn for the flexibility of remote work. We're targeting those who are actively seeking ways to break free from traditional office settings and embrace the freedom of location-independent work. This includes individuals who are passionate about personal growth, financial independence, and the ability to design a lifestyle that aligns with their values and aspirations.

    - User-friendly interface: The web design for our online cinema is designed with ease-of-use in mind. Our interface is intuitive and simple, allowing users to easily navigate the site and find the movies they want to watch.

    - Seamless browsing experience: We understand that users want to spend less time searching for movies and more time watching them. Our web design ensures a seamless browsing experience, with quick-loading pages and easy access to our movie catalog.


    - Mobile responsive design: With more and more people accessing websites on their mobile devices, our web design is mobile responsive, ensuring that users can easily access our site on any device.

    - Personalized recommendations: Our web design includes a personalized recommendation system that suggests movies based on users' viewing history and preferences. This makes it easier for users to find movies that they will love.


    - Comprehensive search functionality: Our web design includes a comprehensive search functionality that allows users to search for movies based on genre, actor, director, and other criteria. This makes it easy for users to find exactly what they are looking for.
